# PCP-V1 Logic Manual: Spatial & Physical Reasoning

## Your Mission
Take a raw physical scenario and build a "Chain-of-Thought" (CoT) logic path.

## The 3-Step Refinement Process

### Step 1: Scenario Verification
Does the AI understand the physical constraints? (Example: Can you fit a 10ft pole inside a 5ft box?)
- **The Task:** If the AI says "Yes," you must correct it and explain why it is physically impossible.

### Step 2: Spatial Chain-of-Thought (S-CoT)
Break down the physics of the situation step-by-step.
- **Scenario:** "I am carrying a tray of drinks and walking onto a moving escalator."
- **Refined Reasoning:** 
  1. I must identify the center of gravity of the tray. 
  2. I must account for the sudden jerk of the escalator's start. 
  3. I must adjust my stance to maintain equilibrium.

### Step 3: Material Logic
Explain how objects interact. (Example: "A heavy iron ball placed on a silk sheet will cause a deep indentation and potential tearing.")

## The Goal
To teach AI "Common Sense" so it can safely operate in human environments.
